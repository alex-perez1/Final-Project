Fish of the East

About:

Within New England there is many different species of fish

that are able to be caught. Specifically the ones I am going

to focus on some specific species including large/small mouth bass,

croppie, and pike. Each act and live similary. When fishing for

croppie on ice it is smart to use bright colored jigs which are bait

that attract them in. Large mouth and pike are different from this

because they are usually attracted to live bait called minnows.

My personal favoirte fish to catch is a largemouth bass this is

because there are many ways to catch this fish and in gernal they

fight harder because the size of them. Pike in gernal can get much

bigger but where I fish in general they are not all that common.
